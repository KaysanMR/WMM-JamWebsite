GENERAL SPREAD QUESTIONS

WHERE CAN I BUY NATURE’S JAM? +

Nature's Jam is waiting for you in many major retailers across Malaysia. To find the nearest one, please visit our store locator.

DO I REFRIGERATE THE JAM? +

Yes, once opened, it’s best to refrigerate to maintain freshness.

WHY IS THERE SOME LIQUID ON TOP OF THE JAM? +

That’s natural fruit juice separating. Just give it a stir! You are welcome to remove it too, but it’s a natural occurrence in a spread that has lesser sugar than a traditional preserve and far more fruit.

WHAT’S IN THE SPREADS

ARE THE JAMS VEGAN/VEGETARIAN? +

Yes! Our fruit spreads meet all categories of vegetarian including vegan. We use no animal ingredients of any kind, and our organic cane sugar is produced without the use of bone-char. Enjoy!

ARE YOUR SPREADS NUT-FREE? +

Absolutely, we do not include any nuts in our spreads.

DO YOUR PRODUCTS CONTAIN ANY ALLERGENS? +

We do not purchase ingredients that may contain allergens. We avoid major allergens but always check the label for specific ingredients for your information.

DO YOUR SPREADS CONTAIN GLUTEN? +

No, all of our spreads are gluten-free and safe for celiac-friendly diets.
