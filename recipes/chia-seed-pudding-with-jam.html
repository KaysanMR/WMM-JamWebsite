Chia Seed Pudding with Jam

Creamy, nutritious chia pudding layered with your favorite Nature's Jam for a fruity breakfast or snack!

  • PREP TIME

    5 minutes

  • CHILL TIME

    2 hours

  • SERVINGS

    2 puddings

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons Nature's Jam (any flavor)
  • Fresh fruit for topping (optional)
Print Recipe

Instructions

  1. In a bowl or jar, whisk together chia seeds, milk, maple syrup, and vanilla.
  2. Let sit for 5 minutes, then stir again to prevent clumping.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight, until thickened.
  4. To serve, layer chia pudding and Nature's Jam in glasses or jars.
  5. Top with fresh fruit if desired and enjoy chilled.
