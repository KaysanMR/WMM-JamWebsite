Jam-filled thumbprint cookies

Sweet, buttery bites with just the right touch of jam — perfect for any occasion.

  • PREP TIME

    15 minutes

  • COOK TIME

    12 minutes

  • SERVINGS

    12 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
Print Recipe

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F) and line a tray with parchment paper.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar until fluffy. Add vanilla.
  3. Add flour and salt gradually to form dough.
  4. Roll dough into 1-inch balls and place on tray.
  5. Make a thumbprint and fill with jam.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes until edges are golden.
  7. Cool before serving or storing.
